Account and Data Deletion Request for The Reviewer

This page provides the mandatory steps and details required to request the deletion of your user account and associated personal data from The Reviewer app, operated by Siddhant Senapati.

1. How to Request Account Deletion (Mandatory Steps)

You can request account deletion directly through the application's support feature:

  1. Navigate to the **Profile** screen in The Reviewer app.
  2. Tap on the **Contact Support** or **Help/Feedback** option.
  3. In the message body, write a clear request stating: **"I request the permanent deletion of my account and all associated data."**
  4. Ensure the email is sent from the address associated with your Google Sign-In account.

Alternatively, you may send the request directly to our support email: siddhant.awaits@gmail.com.

We will confirm your request within 48 hours and process the permanent deletion of your data within 30 days of verification.

2. Data Types Deleted Upon Request

Upon successful verification, the following personal and associated data will be permanently deleted from our live systems (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore):

  • User Account and **Email Address** (from Firebase Authentication).
  • Your **Display Name** and Profile Photo URL.
  • All **Reviews** you have submitted.
  • All **Comments** you have made on other reviews.
  • Your defined **Interests/Categories** list.

3. Data Retained and Retention Period

The following non-personal data may be retained for security and analytics purposes:

  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated usage data collected by Firebase Analytics (which cannot be linked back to you).
  • Legal/Safety Records: Limited data may be retained if required to comply with legal obligations or to prevent abuse/fraud.

No personally identifiable information (PII) will be retained beyond the 30-day processing period once the account deletion request is verified.
