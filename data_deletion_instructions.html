Account and Data Deletion Request for The Reviewer

This page provides the mandatory steps and details required to request the deletion of your user account and associated personal data from The Reviewer app, operated by Siddhant Senapati.

1. How to Request Account Deletion (Mandatory Steps)

To request the permanent deletion of your account and data, you must send an email with the following details:

  • Recipient: siddhant.awaits@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: ACCOUNT DELETION REQUEST - THE REVIEWER
  • Email Body Must Contain:
    • Your full registered **Email Address** (used to sign in with Google).
    • Your **User ID** (if known, otherwise your display name).
    • A clear statement confirming your request for **permanent deletion**.

We will confirm your request within 48 hours and process the deletion within 30 days.

2. Data Types Deleted Upon Request

Upon successful verification of your request, the following personal and associated data will be permanently deleted from our live systems (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore):

  • User Account and **Email Address** (from Firebase Authentication).
  • Your **Display Name** and Profile Photo URL.
  • All **Reviews** you have submitted.
  • All **Comments** you have made on other reviews.
  • Your defined **Interests/Categories** list.

3. Data Retained and Retention Period

In accordance with our data retention policy, the following non-personal or anonymized data may be retained:

  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated usage data collected by Firebase Analytics (which cannot be linked back to you).
  • Legal/Financial Records: Data required to be retained by law (if applicable, e.g., payment records, though this app is free).

No personally identifiable information (PII) will be retained beyond a 30-day processing period once the account deletion request is verified.
