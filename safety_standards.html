Child Safety Standards & Community Guidelines

Effective Date: December 17, 2025

Welcome to The Reviewer. We are committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and inclusive community. These Safety Standards apply to all user-generated content, including reviews, comments, images, and profile information.

The Reviewer has a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). We do not tolerate content that sexualizes minors, promotes the exploitation of children, or endangers child safety in any way.

Our Action Plan: If we identify any content containing CSAM:

  • The content will be removed immediately.
  • The user account will be permanently banned.
  • We will report the content and the user to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement authorities.

1. Prohibited Conduct

To ensure the safety of all users, especially minors, the following behaviors are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment & Bullying: engaging in targeted attacks, cyberbullying, or predatory behavior towards any user.
  • Hate Speech: Promoting violence or hatred based on race, ethnicity, religion, disability, gender, age, or sexual orientation.
  • Inappropriate Content: Posting sexually explicit material, nudity, or graphic violence.
  • Grooming: Any attempt by an adult to build an emotional connection with a minor to gain their trust for sexual abuse or exploitation.

2. Reporting Mechanisms

We empower our community to help us maintain safety. Users can report violations directly through the app or via email.

In-App Reporting:

Every review and comment on The Reviewer has a "Report" button (flag icon). Tap this button to flag content for immediate review by our moderation team.

3. Enforcement and Moderation

We utilize a combination of user reporting and manual administration to enforce these standards. Violations result in actions ranging from content removal to permanent account suspension.
